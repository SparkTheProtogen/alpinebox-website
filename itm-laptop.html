You seem to have read the info QR on my laptop so here's a brief overview:

  • It is an Acer TravelMate B311-31 with 4GB RAM (DDR4 at 2400MT/s, non-removable) and 64GB of eMMC storage (also non-removable), this laptop is powered by an absolute gaming beast of a CPU: the Intel Celeron N4120 running at a maximum clock speed of 2.5GHz and an Intel UHD Graphics 600 iGPU.

  • As for software it is running Arch Linux (btw) with Sway as its window manager, I chose a tiling WM as this screen is tiny, partitioning it between apps and removing the ability to minimise apps ensures I only have apps I am actively using open therefore saving resources AND the mouse is used less as more actions relating to launching, closing and moving applicaions now have keyboard shortcuts assigned to them.
